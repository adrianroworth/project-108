# Flap Design

This document describes the flap parts, printing strategy, and material decisions.

## Recommended materials

- Body: black matte PLA
- Letters/symbols: white PLA or white paint

## Print strategy

- Print 50 flaps first, not 240.
- Use flat orientation where the hinge is strongest.
- Avoid vertical printing unless the design explicitly requires it.
- Use 0.20 mm layer height, 3 walls, 15% gyroid infill, and no supports if possible.

## Validation checks

- Flap fit in the wheel
- Hinge motion smoothness
- Consistent thickness across prints
- Surface finish and colour appearance

## Organisation

- Label batches as they come off the printer using active IDs from `03-symbol-inventory.md`: `N0`-`N9`, `H1`-`H16`.
- Keep two clearly marked passes for each wheel: `Pass A` and `Pass B` (26 symbols each).
- Keep the first batch separate for design review before continuing.
