# Wheel Design

This document covers the split-flap wheel mechanism and wheel layout.

## Goals

- A reliable rotation mechanism for one digit.
- Consistent indexing of flap positions.
- Easy assembly and serviceability.

## Design considerations

- Use a simple geared or friction-driven wheel.
- Provide physical indexing or detents for each flap position.
- Allow the wheel to be removed from the module without disassembling the entire display.

## Prototype priorities

- Validate the wheel geometry with one complete digit.
- Ensure the wheel holds flaps securely while allowing smooth rotation.
- Test the relationship between wheel thickness, flange spacing, and flap hinge clearance.
